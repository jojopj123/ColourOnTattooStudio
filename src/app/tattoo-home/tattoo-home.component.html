About us

Welcome to our tattoo studio located in the vibrant city of Kochi! At our shop, we specialize in a range of services including tattoos, piercings, microblading, lip coloring, and tattoo removal. Our talented artists and technicians are dedicated to providing top-notch services with a focus on creativity, quality, and safety. Whether you're looking to express yourself with a unique tattoo, enhance your features with microblading or lip coloring, or remove an existing tattoo, we are here to bring your vision to life. Visit us today and let us be a part of your tattoo and beauty journey!
